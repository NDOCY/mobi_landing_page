Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Effective Date: 26 August 2025

1. Information We Collect

mobi Suite v1 does not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

2. Payment & Transaction Data

If you use in-app payment features, all payment information is handled securely by third-party payment processors. mobi Suite does not store credit card or bank account information.

3. Analytics

mobi Suite may use third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Supabase) for app performance monitoring and crash reporting. These services may collect technical device information (device type, OS version, app usage statistics) in an anonymous or aggregated form.

4. Sharing of Information

mobi Suite does not share personal information with any third parties. Payment processors may process payment-related information as required to complete transactions.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including secure connections to third-party services. No sensitive payment or personal data is stored by mobi Suite.

6. Your Rights

As mobi Suite v1 collects minimal information, there is currently no user-specific data to access or delete.
